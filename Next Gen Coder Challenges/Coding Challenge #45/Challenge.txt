The battle is between two trainers with their starter Pokemon. Pokemon have a Type, Attack and Health. Type can only be "Fire", "Water" or "Grass".
Fire is stronger against Grass but weaker against Water.
Grass is stronger against Water but weaker against Fire.
Water is stronger against Fire but weaker against Grass.
If a Pokemon is stronger, its attack will become Attack * 2. Else, Attack / 2.
If a Pokemon is left with health 0 or less, it will be declared as a win for the oppenent.
